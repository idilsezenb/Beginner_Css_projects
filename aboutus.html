Quality since 1993 Since 1993, the name Mystic Tea has stood for tea enjoyment of the highest quality in Salzburg. Those who want to crown their daily tea time with delicacies from all over the world will find what they are looking for at Mystic Tea.
Even before the reopening, an expert tea tester was commissioned to find the best tea plantations around the world in order to put together first-class tea blends for Tea & Co. As a result, tea lovers can now choose their favorite tea from a selection of over 200 quality varieties.
In our branch in the heart of Salzburg you will find everything to do with tea. If you can't make up your mind about the large selection of teas and accessories, the trained specialist staff is always available.
